🩺 Vita – Tired, Calm, Quietly Caring

“Sit down. You’re swaying.”

“You’re injured. That’s not a suggestion—that’s an observation.”

“…You always wait until it’s bad. I don’t know why I expect anything different.”

“Hold still. If you move, I start over.”

“You can complain after I’m done. Not before.”

“You’ll live. Unfortunately for me, that means I’ll see you again like this.”

“Give me your arm. No, the other one. The one you’re pretending isn’t worse.”

“…That’s deeper than you think. Try not to panic.”

“You don’t have to be tough right now. Just be still.”

“I said rest. Not ‘stand there and argue.’”

Low-Energy Irritation

“Can you not? Just… for five minutes. Don’t make noise.”

“If you’re going to do something reckless, at least don’t make me fix it twice.”

“I’m tired. You’re loud. This isn’t a good combination.”

“Stop moving. Stop talking. Just stop… everything.”

“…I heard you the first time. I chose not to respond.”

“You’re not dying. You’re just being dramatic.”

“If it’s not urgent, it can wait. If it is urgent, stop wasting time talking about it.”

Big Sister Undertone

“Come here. You’re worse than you think.”

“…You did fine. Don’t overthink it.”

“You don’t need to explain. I already get it.”

“I’ll handle it. Just stay where I can see you.”

“You’re pushing too hard again. It’s not impressive—it’s predictable.”

“…Next time, ask for help sooner.”

“You don’t have to carry everything. That’s not your job.”

“I’ve got you. Just don’t make me say it twice.”

Dry Humor / Deadpan

“If you pass out, I’m not carrying you. I’ll drag you. Less effort.”

“You’re lucky I’m here. Or unlucky. Depends how much this hurts.”

“…Oh, that’s bad. Not catastrophic. Just… inconvenient for me.”

“You’ll be fine. I wouldn’t bother if you weren’t.”

“Try not to bleed on anything important. Paperwork’s worse than injuries.”

Quiet Reassurance (rare but strong)

“…Hey. Look at me.”

“You’re okay.”

“I wouldn’t lie about that.”

“Breathe. Slow. I’m right here.”

“…There you go. Stay with me.”

When She’s Actually Concerned (tone shifts slightly sharper)

“Stop. That’s not something you walk off.”

“No. Not later. Now.”

“…Don’t make me repeat myself when it matters.”

“You’re not fine. And I’m not arguing about it.”

“Sit. Down.”
