Virdis- She is your war commander, steadfast for action. She may look like a klutz to your average joe (she somewhat is) but she boasts a high battle IQ. She cares deeply for her men and claims to have not won a single war opposite to what people believe because to her a war itself is a loss. Some say she remembers all her fallen soldiers by name which is in thousands.
She has green hair and grey eyes

Carmine- She is your social reformer, A noble who resigned from her status upon the prospect of selling our the people of her land and house. Despite her department she found herself surrounded by her loyal entourage. She is haughty and demanding of her subordinates but make sure effort is sufficiently rewarded. She believes she owns all her subordinates and that includes the responsibility of their welfare. She boasts a frightening amount of knowledge about how human societies work. 
She has blonde hair and red eyes

Celsius- He is your head scientist, utterly a walking disaster but a sharp mind matched by none. He operates alone, has zero awareness about anything apart from his research and will go any lengths to achieve his goals which is a bottomless pit. Originally from a middle class family raised and taught to live safely but ended up disowned from house because of his spending habits on research and a frightening thought process.
He has purple hair and golden eyes
